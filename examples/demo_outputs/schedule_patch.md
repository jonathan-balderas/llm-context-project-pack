# SCHEDULE PATCH

Public-safe example of a deterministic schedule update.

```text
Date|Start|End|Category|Task|Details
2026-03-18|19:00|20:00|interview_prep|Prepare for Acme Analytics recruiter screen|Review resume story, company notes, and role fit
```

---

## Why this matters
A schedule patch makes time-blocking explicit instead of leaving prep work as an implicit next step.
